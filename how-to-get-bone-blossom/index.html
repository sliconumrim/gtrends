how to get bone blossom

Bone Blossom, the coveted seed in the game has been a topic of discussion among players for quite some time now. To obtain this highly sought-after seed, one must participate in the Grow a Garden Prehistoric Dino event quests. The central event hub, where Blaire the NPC resides, holds the key to unlocking the Bone Blossom seed. By interacting with Blaire, you will be presented with your Dino quests and a weekly task completion bar at the bottom of the screen. Completing each task rewards you with individual rewards, and as you progress through the week, the completion bar fills up. Once the meter is filled, the coveted Bone Blossom seed will be yours. The Bone Blossom seed has been revealed to possess 'Transcendent' rarity, making it a highly valuable addition to any player's garden collection. Its unique appearance bears resemblance to a Peach and Nectarine, setting it apart from other blossoms in the game. Prior to its release, hints were given by Jandel that this new blossom-type seed would be superior to Candy Blossom, the previously held title of 'best' seed in the game. With Bone Blossom confirmed as the better option, players are eager to get their hands on it and experience its benefits for themselves.
